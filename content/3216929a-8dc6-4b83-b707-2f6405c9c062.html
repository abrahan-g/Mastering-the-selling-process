Strategic interviewing within the 7-step sales process is crucial for gathering deep insights. Asking well-prepared questions helps understand client needs and opens opportunities for growth. For instance, questions like “What are your spending habits on essential items?” or “How do you determine product specification needs?” are pivotal. Using empathy and understanding client backgrounds lead to effective follow-ups and pinpointing growth potential. Practice questions with peers or in front of a manager to refine your technique and handle objections with care.
