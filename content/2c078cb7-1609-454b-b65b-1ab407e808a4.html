Selecting and targeting the right prospects is foundational for preparing successful sales meetings. Key criteria include interacting with the correct decision-makers, evaluating credit-worthiness, and understanding purchasing behavior. Building on the concept of 'right-to-win customers', the aim is to streamline efforts toward prospects with the highest potential return. Efficient preparation lays the groundwork for ensuing stages, enhancing the quality of interviews and presentations, and increasing the probability of closing deals successfully.
