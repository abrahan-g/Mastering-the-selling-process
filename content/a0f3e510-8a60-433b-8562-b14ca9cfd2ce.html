What key question should be asked to handle objections?

"If we properly understand your objections to growing with us, and we lay out a detailed plan, would you be willing to commit?"

How should one respond to customer objections?

Do NOT push back. Instead, ask follow-up questions to fully understand and use empathy to acknowledge their concerns.

What's the process for recognizing different customer profiles?

Identify if they are credit-worthy, spec-driven, or decision makers through strategic questioning and background research.
